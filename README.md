# PartyRock--ATS-Helper
Welcome to ATS Helper — your complete AI-powered resume and interview prep toolkit.

##Link: https://partyrock.aws/u/Nehal/Jv77og6PI/ATS-Helper

**How to Use:** Upload your resume, paste the job description, select your target region, and the app generates everything you need.

---

**📄 RESUME OPTIMIZATION SECTION**
• **Keyword Extraction** — pulls all important keywords from the job description
• **ATS Optimized Resume** — rewrites your resume to pass ATS systems (preserves all your real facts)
• **Formatting Guidelines** — tells you how to format your resume for your region
• **Summary of Changes** — explains what was changed and why
• **Validation Report** — verifies nothing was fabricated or removed

**💼 HIRING & CAREER DEVELOPMENT**
• **Hiring Potential** — realistic assessment of your interview chances + strengths/gaps
• **Learning Plan** — structured skill development roadmap (0-3, 3-6, 6+ months)
• **Professional Certifications Roadmap** — which certifications to get and when

**🏢 COMPANY & JOB INSIGHTS**
• **About the Company** — deep analysis of company, culture, tech stack, and SWOT
• **Technical Glossary** — explains every technical term in simple language

**📝 APPLICATION MATERIALS**
• **Cover Letter** — tailored professional cover letter based on your real experience

**🎤 INTERVIEW PREPARATION**
• **Interview Preparation Guide** — complete guide with focus topics + strategies and tips
• **Interview Questions Bank** — 30 scenario-based questions + detailed answers for top 6 most critical ones

---

**⚠️ Important:** All outputs preserve your original facts — nothing is invented or exaggerated.
